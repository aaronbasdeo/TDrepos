Understand curation of customer-generated content

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home > Reviews & Questions

Marketplace Manager presents you with a time-stamped list of all newly submitted product reviews and product questions, updated in real time as users submit them. You can then review each post individually and either approve or deny it. Or, you can bulk-approve the full set of all pending posts.

When denying a post, you have the option to enter a reason for your denial.

The author of any denied post receives notification of its denial through email. And, when you have entered any explanation for the denial, the notification message cites it in its entirety.
