Product profile pages

A product’s marketing profile might include and emphasize any of several kinds of information.

Parts of a product profile

Features and benefits

Read a summary preview of the described product’s competitive benefits and features. Learn about key use cases and understand what sets a product apart from its competitors.

Featured customers

Learn which marquee customers the marketplace provider has specified that developers can cite, whose use of the described product serve as implicit testimonials. Developer claims of product effectiveness and high quality are bolstered when industry leaders prefer their products.

Reviews

On a marketplace that permits its consumers to write and post product reviews, the Reviews tab highlights opinions that they have shared about the described product.

 NOTE:  The marketplace provider sets policies that determine who can review a product and whether reviews are moderated before publication.

Ratings

Ratings from other real people help to shape purchasing decisions and encourage registered users to describe their own experiences. Anyone who writes a review can also submit a star rating to rank the product from 1 to 5. An averaged star rating is added to the profile page and appears next to the app in search results.

 NOTE:  The marketplace provider sets policies that determine who can rate a product and how many ratings must be submitted for a product before its ratings are averaged and the average is published.

Product Q&A forum

Consumers ask questions about the described product, and the product’s own developers answer these questions.

 NOTE:  The marketplace provider sets policies that determine whether product profile pages can include such forums.

Product support details

Read about the described product’s support options and minimum system requirements.

Product resources

External videos, articles, and web seminars can help consumers to consider a product in context.

Product editions and pricing

Consumers can learn about their options when looking to order an application. They can compare plans and pricing and see any special offers before placing an order.

Screenshots

Consumers can get a closer look at the product with screenshots that show the product in action.

Demo

See the product in action through a video or demo.
