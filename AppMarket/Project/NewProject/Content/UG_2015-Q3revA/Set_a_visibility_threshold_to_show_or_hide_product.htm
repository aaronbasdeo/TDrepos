Set a visibility threshold to show or hide product ratings

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

Scroll to the Reviews & Questions area.

Enter a number in the ratings must be given before a product rating is visible on the marketplace field.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
