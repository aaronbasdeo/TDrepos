Understand product catalog curation

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Products

Catalog management features of Marketplace Manager help you to build your business by ensuring a product inventory that is as compelling as you can possibly make it, presented in its most flattering light. You can:

Craft product categories, industries, and attributes to target your most promising customer segments. Such tags are fully searchable.

Pick and choose the most exciting products to populate your categories.

Run tests against product integrations before they go live.

Approve or reject the marketing profiles and pricing plans (editions) that your vendors prepare for their products.

Accept or reject incoming publication requests from known developers.

Drive demand with carousels that highlight featured products, popular products, and staff picks.

Mark upcoming additions to your product catalog as Coming Soon.

Mark catalog products as Under Maintenance, as needed.

Remove from your inventory any products that don’t perform well.

Layout tools help you to shape the presentation and organization of product listing pages in your catalog.

Moreover, you can expand your developer community how you see fit and add yet more cloud services to your marketplace from the AppDirect network catalog, which features hundreds of [how many more? who owns the canonical data and where can writers see it?] fully integrated products.
