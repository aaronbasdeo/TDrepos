 TIP:  We developed this material for use by someone with the channel administrator user role. When you lack this role assignment, at least some described features are not available to you. To learn which user roles you have on a marketplace instance, go to https://{host}/profile and read through the User Information area on the Profile tab. (Note, however, that you see this information about your personal account at /profile only when you are a company administrator. Registered users who lack the company administrator role cannot see their role assignments at /profile.) Then, to learn which features pertain to your assigned roles, see User role basics.
