Work with your invoices ledger

You can browse, filter, and search invoice records.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Invoices

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

Click past Billing | Invoices.

(Optional) Enter a query in the Search field that matches all or part of an invoice ID.

Then, either press Enter or click the magnifying glass icon.
When your query matches one or more records, we trim the ledger to expose only the matches.

(Optional) Click Show Filters to expand a toolbar whose controls help you to filter which invoices the table should show. Then, do either or both of the following.

 NOTE:  Selections take effect immediately, and each effect is additive.

 

Do you want to filter the displayed records by their payment status?

Apply a payment status filter

Choose an option from the Status list.

Paid — List only the invoices that your payment system has marked as paid.

Unpaid — List only the invoices that your payment system has marked as unpaid.

Carried — List only the invoices that your payment system has marked as carried.

Do you want to filter the displayed records by when your marketplace created their cited invoices?

Apply a creation date filter

Define the start and end of an invoice generation date range by entering dates manually in the paired date-entry fields.

OR

Click a field’s corresponding calendar icon () to expose the date-picker widget for populating that field.

 NOTE:  When you enter dates manually, use the calendar date notation syntax that is standard in your locale and which your marketplace is configured to use — either M/D/YY) or D/M/YY.

(Optional) Do either of the following.

Do you want to see when your marketplace first issued a given invoice?

Check the date of one invoice

Submit a search query where the relevant invoice ID is your search string.

When the Invoices table is refreshed, check the value in its Date column.

Do you want to export some or all invoice records to a CSV file?

Download spreadsheet-ready invoice records

Search, sort, and filter results until they are correctly narrowed for your purposes.

Click Download CSV.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
