Approve or deny customer-generated content

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Reviews & Questions

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

Click through Home | Reviews & Questions.

OR
Go to https://{marketplace}/channel/marketplace?#approve, where {marketplace} is the DNS-resolvable server address.

(Optional) Click Approve N () to approve all entries simultaneously, where N is the number of posts that await your decision.

(Optional) Approve or disapprove entries one at a time by clicking their respective  (Approve) and  (Deny) buttons.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
