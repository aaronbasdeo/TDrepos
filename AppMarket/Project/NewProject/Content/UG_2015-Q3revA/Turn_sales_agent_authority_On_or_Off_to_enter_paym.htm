Turn sales agent authority On or Off to enter payment details

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

Scroll to the Customer Roles area.

Operate the Allows Sales Agents to enter payment details on behalf of a customer checkbox, next to the Sales Agent Payment label, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
Sales agents are empowered to enter payment details on behalf of consumers during assisted sales.

OR

Clear the checkbox.
Sales agents are not empowered to enter payment details on behalf of consumers during assisted sales.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
