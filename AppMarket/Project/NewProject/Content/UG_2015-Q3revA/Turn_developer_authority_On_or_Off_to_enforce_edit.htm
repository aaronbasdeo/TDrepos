Turn developer authority On or Off to enforce edition restrictions

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

Scroll to the Subscriptions area.

Operate the Enable edition level subscription restrictions checkbox, next to the Edition Restrictions label, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
Developers can see and use options in Developer Center to set advanced contract stipulations for any of their product editions.

OR

Clear the checkbox.
Developers cannot impose any advanced contract stipulations per product edition.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
