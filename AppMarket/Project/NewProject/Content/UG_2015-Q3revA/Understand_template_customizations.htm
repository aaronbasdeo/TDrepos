Understand template customizations

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Custom UI | Customer Emails

You control and personalize the content in every sent message, through your use of a given template’s supported parameters. Furthermore, you can enable or disable any optional template, configure certain templates to send message copies automatically to a sales agent, and populate the “unsubscribe” text that certain templates inject into their generated messages.
