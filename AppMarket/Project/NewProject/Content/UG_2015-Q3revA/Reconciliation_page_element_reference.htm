Marketplace Share (column)

Exactly the revenue sharing percentage that your company receives as the marketplace provider when consumer contracts are sold, which grant access to the cited product.

Vendor Share (column)

Exactly the revenue sharing percentage that its vendor company receives when consumer contracts are sold, which grant access to the cited product.

AppDirect Share (column)

Payout Ownership (column)

Cites by name which party (AppDirect, the marketplace provider, or the product vendor) is contractually liable for disbursing proceeds to all other involved parties whenever the cited product drives revenue.
