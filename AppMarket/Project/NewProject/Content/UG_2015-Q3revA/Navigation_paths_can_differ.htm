Navigation paths can differ

The visual style theme that is applied to a given marketplace can change its top-level UI navigation paths. In addition, UI element names and labels can differ per marketplace in their nomenclature, their capitalization, their spelling, or in any other way. Also:

Users with the designer role can rearrange and hide parts of the UI.

Custom workflows can be injected per marketplace, upon request, moving standard features and functions from one place to another or introducing new features and functions.

Certain products are sold via, or provisioned via, product-specific or vendor-specific workflows that differ from the workflows that other products employ.
