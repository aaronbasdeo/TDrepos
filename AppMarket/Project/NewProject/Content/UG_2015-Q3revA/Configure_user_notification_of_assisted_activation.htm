Configure user notification of assisted activation <<SUPERUSER FEATURE>>

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

Scroll to the Customer Roles area.

Operate the Send activation email when a Sales Agent creates a customer checkbox, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
Users whose accounts were created during assisted sales receive email messages that invite them to activate their accounts. [Must they click through these messages to become activated?]

OR

Clear the checkbox.
These users do not receive any such message. [Is the account considered active because a sales agent registered it?]

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
