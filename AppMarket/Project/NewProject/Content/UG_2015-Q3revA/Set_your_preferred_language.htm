Set your preferred language

The user interface for a given marketplace might be available in only one language or might support as many as a dozen languages. Although you can switch between any of the languages that your marketplace supports, its customizations might affect where and how you choose.

 TIP:  Illustrations in this topic might not be an exact match for your preferred marketplace. Every storefront has its own set of supported languages, its own color scheme, and its own customizations.

 

 NOTE:  Marketplaces that support only one language do not have any option to switch between languages.

Procedure

Go to https://{marketplace}, where {marketplace} is the fully qualified and DNS resolvable server name for your marketplace instance.

Depending on the visual design of your marketplace, do one of the following.

Click a language in the page footer.

Choose a language from the languages list.

Click the button or toggle whose label cites your preferred language.

(Optional) Reload your browser, as needed.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
