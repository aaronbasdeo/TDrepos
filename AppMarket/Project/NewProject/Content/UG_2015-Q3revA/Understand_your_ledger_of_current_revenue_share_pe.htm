Understand your ledger of current revenue share percentages per product

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Reconciliations (Revenue Shares)

Your revenue shares ledger shows an almost-live representation of revenue sharing percentage agreements per product, between your marketplace and your revenue partners. The ledger does not refresh automatically in your browser, so its representation of these agreements is only as recent as when your browser loaded it.

The ledger renders seven columns in its UI version and 14 columns in its exportable CSV version. It is searchable by product name and supports filtering by activity date [revenue sharing activity? Some other kind of activity?]. [By ‘activity date,’ do we mean the date when someone edited percentages? Or do we mean the date when reconciliation took place?]
