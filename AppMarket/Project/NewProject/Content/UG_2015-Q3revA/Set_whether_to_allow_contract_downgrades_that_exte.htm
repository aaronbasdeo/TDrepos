Set whether to allow contract downgrades that extend the enddate

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

Scroll to the Contracts area.

Operate the Allow increase in contract duration within edition to take priority over price in downgrade scenario checkbox, next to the Contract Restrictions label, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
Users are now authorized sitewide to substitute a less powerful edition for a more powerful one, or a less expensive edition for a more expensive one. as long as the lesser edition enforces a longer subscription period than the original contract enforced.

OR

Clear the checkbox.
User authorization to downgrade an ACTIVE subscription contract is determined by the contract’s own stipulations, rather than the life span of the downgraded contract.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
