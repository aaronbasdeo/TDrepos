Understand Marketplace Manager

Marketplace Manager is an environment to populate one AppDirect-powered marketplace with third-party products and services, configure its settings and policies, and administer essentially all aspects of its daily operation as a business.

Marketplace Manager includes the dashboards, reports, logs, analytics, and other tools to understand and shape the relationships between a given marketplace and its many constituents,  including resellers, developers, and consumers.

Many AppDirect instances include Marketplace Manager, but it is not a baseline UI portal. Instead, access to it is restricted to the small subset of registered users who are authorized to see and use it, on the AppDirect plans that include it. The relevant user role is channel administrator.
