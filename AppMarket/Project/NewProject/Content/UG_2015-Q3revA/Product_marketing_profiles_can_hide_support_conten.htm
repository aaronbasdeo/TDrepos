Product marketing profiles can hide support content

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can prevent product marketing profile pages sitewide from rendering any content that would otherwise populate their Support tabs. This content could include URLs, email addresses, telephone numbers, and technical documentation PDFs.

The business case to hide such content could be, for example, that your organization is the sole vendor on your marketplace and delivers its support services elsewhere. Or the business logic could be that your organization sells only the third-party products for which it sells multi-tiered technical support service. Only your organization can determine when or if to hide support content that would otherwise be visible on product marketing profile pages.
