Why to collect billing details for free-trial products

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that collects billing details when consumers place orders for free-trial product editions. When the behavior is turned Off, the checkout process is simplified for free-trial product editions.
