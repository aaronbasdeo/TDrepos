Understand company administrator authority to import products <<ALPHA FEATURE FOR CLOUD MGMT STE>>

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that empowers company administrators to import, manage, and assign products that their companies obtained elsewhere, which are billed outside of your marketplace. When this behavior is turned On, your marketplace has more “stickiness” to engage and retain its customers.

The AppDirect catalog of importable products contains more than 1,000 popular offerings.
