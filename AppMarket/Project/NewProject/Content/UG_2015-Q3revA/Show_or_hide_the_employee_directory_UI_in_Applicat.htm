Show or hide the employee directory UI in Application Manager

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

Scroll to the Customer UI area.

Operate the Enable user profile and company profile page checkbox, next to the User Profiles label, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
The users who are associated with a company can see and use the profile pages that describe them and their associated company.

OR

Clear the checkbox.
Company and user profile pages are hidden from company administrators, billing administrators, and end-users.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
