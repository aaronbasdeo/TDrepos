Product marketing profiles can hide product ratings

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that hides all user-submitted star ratings for a product until the number of submitted ratings meets or exceeds a minimum threshold. For example, you might consider it unreasonable to show fewer than 10 ratings per product. When the behavior is turned Off, all submitted star ratings are visible immediately.

 NOTE:  This feature is not relevant to you unless your marketplace is configured to show usergenerated content.
