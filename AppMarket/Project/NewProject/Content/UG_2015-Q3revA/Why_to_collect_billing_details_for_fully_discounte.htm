Why to collect billing details for fully discounted products

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that collects billing details when consumers apply a 100% discount toward their order of any product whose contract has a defined end-date. [Do we mean, literally, a 100% discount, or do we mean any discount whose face value amounts to at least the full price of whatever was ordered?] When the behavior is turned Off, the checkout process is simplified for any fully discounted order that has a defined end-date.

 TIP:  We never collect billing details when someone applies a 100% discount to the order of any product that lacks a defined end-date. For context, we often call these “one-time orders.”
