Set an email address for developer technical support

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Vendors

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Vendors.

Scroll to the Vendor Support area.

Enter a correctly configured and active email address in the Developer Support Email field.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
