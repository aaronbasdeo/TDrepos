Understand your payouts ledger

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Reconciliations (Payouts)

Your payouts ledger shows an almost-live representation of revenue sharing payouts as your marketplace pays its vendors. The ledger does not refresh automatically in your browser, so its representation of reconciliations is only as recent as when your browser loaded it.

The ledger renders seven columns in its UI version and eight columns in its exportable CSV version. It is searchable by vendor name and supports filtering by:

payout status

creation date
