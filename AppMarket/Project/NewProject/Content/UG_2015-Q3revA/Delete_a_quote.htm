Delete a quote

You can delete saved quotes that your sales agents generate.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Quotes> {quote} > [gear icon ()] > Delete a Quote

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Quotes.

Click through Billing | Quotes.

Click a quote to see its details on the Manage Quote page.

Choose Delete Quote from the gear icon ().

The Delete Quote popup opens.

Click Delete.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
