Why to enable sales agent authority to bypass customer approval

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can limit sales agent authority to conduct assisted sales without a target user’s explicit consent. There are three mutually exclusive alternatives.

Sales agents can complete any assisted sale without a target user’s consent or involvement.

OR

Sales agents must obtain explicit consent from the target user, who completes each order.

OR

Sales agents have personal discretion to choose on a case-by-case basis whether it is appropriate to obtain a target user’s consent.

 NOTE:  In relation to assisted sales, the “sales agent” designation can mean someone with the reseller user role, when your marketplace uses the reseller service, or someone with the sales support user role.
