Work with your payments ledger

You can browse, filter, and search the records of payments made and refunds issued.

 NOTE:  Although a UI toggle above the Payments table labels its two view states Overview and Details (), the latter view is not what we mean in this guide when we mention “payment details.” Instead, we mean the modal popup that opens — which describes only one payment record in detail — when you click through one row in the Payments table, regardless of that table’s toggled view state when you click it.

Relative to your payments ledger:

  •   The Overview toggle exposes seven values per payment record.
  •   The Details toggle exposes 11 values.
  •   These two views share five of their values in common. So:

         –   Two values per record are unique to the Overview view.
         –   Six values per record are unique to the Details view.

Choose whichever view exposes more values that matter to you.

  •   Values that both views expose are Payment ID, Date, Customer, Vendor, and Total.
  •   Unique values in the Overview view are Payment Method and Payment Result.
  •   Unique values in the Details view are Payout Status, Invoice ID, Product, Edition, Description,
       and Vendor Share.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Payments

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

Click past Billing | Payments.

(Optional) Enter a query in the Search field that matches all or part of a payment ID.

Then, either press Enter or click the magnifying glass icon.
When your query matches one or more records, we trim the ledger to expose only the matches.

(Optional) Click Show Filters to expand a toolbar whose controls help you to filter which invoices the table should show. Then, do any or all of the following.

 NOTE:  Selections take effect immediately, and each effect is additive.

OVERVIEW VIEW

Do you want to filter the displayed records by payment method?

Filter the overview view by the buyer’s payment instrument

Make sure that the toggle state is correctly Overview.

Choose an option from the Payment Method list to see only records that use the specified method.

On your marketplace, the list’s options might differ from these:

Do you want to filter the displayed records by whether they represent payments or refunds?

Filter the overview view by income or outlay

Make sure that the toggle state is correctly Overview.

Choose an option from the Type list to see records that represent payments exclusively or refunds exclusively.

Do you want to filter the displayed records by the outcome of payment attempts?

Filter the overview view by payment attempt result

Make sure that the toggle state is correctly Overview.

Choose an option from the Payment Result list to see records that represent one or all possible outcomes.

DETAILS VIEW

Do you want to filter the displayed records y payment method?

Filter the details view by the buyer’s payment instrument

Make sure that the toggle state is correctly Details.

Choose an option from the Payment Method list to see only records that use the specified method.

On your marketplace, the list’s options might differ from these:

Do you want to filter the displayed records by whether they represent payments or refunds?

Filter the details view by income or outlay

Make sure that the toggle state is correctly Details.

Choose an option from the Type list to see records that represent payments exclusively or refunds exclusively.

Do you want to filter the displayed records by invoice line type?

Filter the details view by the applied charge type

Make sure that the toggle state is correctly Details.

Choose an option from the Line Type list to see records that represent one or all kinds of possibly applied charges.

Do you want to filter the displayed records by whether a 100 percent discount was applied?

Filter the details view by excluding all fully discounted products

Make sure that the toggle state is correctly Details.

Check the Exclude... check box.

Do you want to filter the displayed records by when your marketplace processed the payment/refund?

Filter the details view by when payments (or refunds) were processed

Make sure that the toggle state is correctly Details.

Define the start and end of a payment processing date range by entering dates manually in the paired date-entry fields.

OR

Click a field’s corresponding calendar icon () to expose the date-picker widget for populating that field.

 NOTE:  When you enter dates manually, use the calendar date notation syntax that is standard in your locale and which your marketplace is configured to use — either M/D/YY) or D/M/YY.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
