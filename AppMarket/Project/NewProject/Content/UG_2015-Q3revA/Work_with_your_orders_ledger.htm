Work with your orders ledger

You can browse, filter, and search order records.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Orders

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

Click past Billing | Orders.

(Optional) Toggle your preferred view: Products () or Editions ().

Click Show Filters to expand a toolbar whose controls help you to for filter which orders the table should show.

The appearance and options here differ according to whether you clicked the Products view or the Editions view.

The Products view () exposes these controls:

The Editions view () exposes these controls:

Do any combination of the following, until you find the orders that matter to you.

 NOTE:  Selections take effect immediately, and each effect is additive.

 

Do you want to find orders that match all or part of an order number?

(Optional) Search by order number

Enter your query in the Search field, and then either press Enter or click the magnifying glass icon.

When your query matches one or more records, we trim the ledger to expose only these matches.

Do you want to filter the displayed records by their order state?

(Optional) Filter orders by their state

Choose an order state from the Status list.

State options differ between the Products view (left) and the Editions view (right), as follows.

Do you want to filter the displayed records by whether the ordered edition is billable?

(Optional) Filter orders by whether they are billable

Choose an option from the Cost list.

Do you want to filter the displayed records by when orders were placed?

(Optional) Filter orders by when customers first placed them

Define the start and end of an order placement date range by entering dates manually in the paired date-entry fields.

OR

Click a field’s corresponding calendar icon () to expose the date-picker widget for populating that field.

 NOTE:  When you enter dates manually, use the calendar date notation syntax that is standard in your locale and which your marketplace is configured to use — either M/D/YY) or D/M/YY.

Do you want to filter the displayed records so that they include even orders that are pending?

(Optional) Stop filtering out pending orders

Check the Show Pending checkbox.

 NOTE:  The Show Pending checkbox is visible only when your Orders table is in its Products view ().

(Optional) Click a table row to show more in-depth details from its underlying record.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
