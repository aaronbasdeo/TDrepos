Log in to a typical marketplace

This procedure supposes that no higher-level organization than the provider oversees your marketplace.

Procedure

Go to https://{marketplace}/login, where {marketplace} is the fully qualified and DNS resolvable server name for your marketplace instance.

Enter your username and password in the fields provided.

 NOTE:  Some marketplace providers enforce the case-sensitivity of usernames, but others do not. To prevent needless complications on a marketplace that imposes this requirement, always enter your username as if case-sensitivity matters.

Press Enter.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
