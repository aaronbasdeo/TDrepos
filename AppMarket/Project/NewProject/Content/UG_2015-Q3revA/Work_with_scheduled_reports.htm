Work with scheduled reports

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Reports

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

Click through Home | Reports.

(Optional) Click Show Filters to expand a toolbar whose controls help you to filter which reports the table should show. Then, do any or all of the following.

 NOTE:  Filter selections take effect immediately, and each effect is additive.

 

Do you want to filter the reports list by report type?

Apply a report type filter

Choose an option from the Report Type list.

Do you want to filter the reports list by the reporting period start date?

Apply a reporting period start date filter

Limit the oldest record that a report should include by setting its date range manually in the paired date-entry fields labeled ‘Start Date between.’

OR

Click a field’s corresponding calendar icon () to expose the date-picker widget for populating that field.

 NOTE:  When you enter dates manually, use the calendar date notation syntax that is standard in your locale and which your marketplace is configured to use — either M/D/YY) or D/M/YY.

Do you want to filter the reports list by the reporting period end date?

Apply a reporting period end date filter

Limit the newest record that a report should include by setting its date range manually in the paired date-entry fields labeled ‘End Date between.’

OR

Click a field’s corresponding calendar icon () to expose the date-picker widget for populating that field.

 NOTE:  When you enter dates manually, use the calendar date notation syntax that is standard in your locale and which your marketplace is configured to use — either M/D/YY) or D/M/YY.

Click a report to download it for offline processing.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
