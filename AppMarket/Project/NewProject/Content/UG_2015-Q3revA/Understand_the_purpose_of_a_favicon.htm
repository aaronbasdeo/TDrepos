Understand the purpose of a favicon

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

A favicon is a very small image that acts as a sort of miniaturized website logo, which browsers can render:

in their location bar

in their title bar

next to a page title in their bookmarks and, sometimes, history

on the tab or pinned tab where a website loads in a tabbed browser

You can set the favicon for your marketplace.
