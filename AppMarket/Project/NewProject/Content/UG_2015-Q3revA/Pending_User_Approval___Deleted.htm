The sequence of transitions in Scenario 17

The chronology of transitions in this scenario is as follows.

Order and subscription state transitions in Scenario 17

 

A newly placed order starts its life in the PENDING USER APPROVAL state when a reseller (or other authorized representative of your marketplace) has placed an order on behalf of a registered user.

There is no subscription state at this stage. A subscription does not yet exist.

 

The order state can transition immediately to DELETED at this point only when the target user (on whose behalf your authorized representative placed the order) refuses to authorize it.

There is no subscription state at this stage. No subscription was created.
