Overview

Many possible states and transitions, in many possible combinations, might apply to a purchase order on your AppDirect-powered marketplace. While the all-inclusive state diagram (Figure A-1) in this overview topic can appear overwhelming at first glance — given its 14 states and their 23 additive transitions across 39 possible scenarios that you might observe in a production deployment — subsequent topics illustrate and explain the scenarios plainly. The real-world scenarios are all individually straightforward.

Color use in state diagrams tell you whether a given purchase order state is most naturally a beginning, imminent to a steady state, a steady state, an ending, or briefly interstitial.
