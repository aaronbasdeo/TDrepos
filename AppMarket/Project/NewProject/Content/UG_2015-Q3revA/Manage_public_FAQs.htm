Manage public FAQs

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Custom UI | FAQs

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Custom UI | FAQs.

As needed:

Click Add Another FAQ Category, and then respond to the prompts.

Click Add Another Question, and then respond to the prompts.

Enter answers to questions.

Organize FAQs into FAQ categories.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
