??? hide channel user information from non-channel users ???

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

Scroll to the Customer UI area.

Operate the Hide channel user names and email addresses from customers checkbox, next to the Channel User Information label, as follows. [Why, where, and how would a marketplace UI disclose anything at all, ever, about any registered user from Company X to anyone whose association is with a different company altogether? Aren’t registered users always sandboxed by their current company association context, even when they maintain multiple associations simultaneously? ... Are product marketing profile pages an exception, somehow? The user-submitted product reviews and product questions are the only place I can imagine where we might sometimes deem this disclosure appropriate.]

Check the checkbox.
User names and email addresses from your company, the marketplace provider, are hidden from all users who are associated with other companies than yours.

OR

Clear the checkbox.
The information about users at your company is not hidden from users at other companies.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
