Configure whether user account activation collects a phone number

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

Scroll to the Customer UI area.

Operate the Customers must enter a phone number to activate their account checkbox, next to the Activation label, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
Registered users must enter a telephone number during the account activation flow. [Would we rather this were the company telephone number, given that companies are the billable parties? If so, how do we manage this record when a user maintains multiple company associations simultaneously? Do we prompt for a different number each time that a new user-company association is established?]

OR

Clear the checkbox.
The account activation flow does not collect any telephone number.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
