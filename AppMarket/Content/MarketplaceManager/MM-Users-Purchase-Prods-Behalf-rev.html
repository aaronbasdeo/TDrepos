Purchase products on behalf of a user

In most cases, individual end users do not purchase their own products. Instead, products are purchased on their behalf by individuals with one of the following roles: Sales Support, Reseller, or Marketplace Manager. 

The following procedure explains how Marketplace Managers can purchase products on behalf of users.

To purchase products on behalf of a user

  1. Go to Manage > Marketplace> Dashboard > Home | Users. The Users page opens.

  2. Click the user for whom you want to purchase a product. The user's details page opens.

  3. Click Make Purchase. The Choose a Product to Purchase dialog box appears.

  4. In the left pane, click the application you want to purchase on behalf of the user. It appears on the right side. 

    (placeholder for image)

  5. (Optional) If you have already discussed the purchase with the user and received his or her approval, click Additional Options to expand the dialog box, and select the checkbox under the Already Received Approval heading. 

    When you indicate that a purchase has been pre-approved by the user, you can complete the purchase without the need for approval by the user, and the order completion and confirmation process are slightly different. Those differences are outlined in steps 10 and 11, below.

  6. Click Start Purchase. A purchase confirmation page opens, on which you can select options or add-ons.

  7. (Optional) Choose purchase options and add-ons. Depending on the product, options can include changing the number of users, modifying the price, changing from a free trial to a purchased version of the product (or the reverse), and applying a discount code.

    If you are not ready to place the order, you can click Save a Quote in the top-left corner and complete the order later. See View and edit quotes to learn more.

  8. Click Continue. The Complete Billing Details page opens.

  9. Select one of the following payment methods:

    • PayPal—You must have the user's credentials to complete the purchase.
    • Credit card—If the user has not yet entered credit card information for his or her account, ensure that you have all of the details required to complete the transaction (see the fields in the following image).
    • Manual payment—The payment takes place outside of the AppDirect platform. As the Channel Administrator, it is your responsibility to coordinate payment directly with the user.

    When you select a payment method, corresponding billing information fields become visible, which you must fill out in order to proceed with the purchase.

However, if the user wants to use a different credit card than one previously used, he or she must update the credit card information from within his or her own account.

  10. Click Continue. The purchase confirmation page opens. It contains order details such as product name, price, and so on. The appearance of the page and the next step in the purchase process are different depending on whether or not the purchase was pre-approved (see step 5).

  • If the purchase was pre-approved, a Place Order button appears at the bottom right of the page.
  • If the purchase was not pre-approved, a Send Approval Email button appears at the top of the page.
  • Step 11. Take one of the following actions:
  • If the purchase was pre-approved, select the terms and conditions checkbox and click Place Order. The user receives a confirmation email and the completed purchase appears in the Orders list on the user's details page, and the order appears in the Pending Purchases list. Simultaneously, the user receives an emal that contains a purchase approval link. The user must click the link to access the order confirmation p age, select the purchase approval and terms and conditions checkboxes, and click Place Order to complete the product purchase. The user receives a purchase receipt through email.
  • If the purchase was not pre-approved, click Send Approval Email. You are returned to the user details page, and the order appears in the Pending Purchases list. Simultaneously, the user receives an email that contains a purchase approval link. The user must click the link to access the order confirmation page, select the purchase approval and terms and conditions checkboxes, and click Place Order to complete the product purchase. The user receives a purchase receipt through email.
In some cases, when a product is purchased, you can include licenses or seats for additional users. In those cases, the initial user, for whom the product was purchased, is able to use the service immediately, but you must assign the application to the additional users before they can access it. See Assign applications to learn more.
