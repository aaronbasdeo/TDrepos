Company Synchronization

Note: The Company Synchronization feature is in closed preview.

The Company synchronization (Company Sync) feature makes onboarding new companies into the AppDirect Marketplace easier. It allows Channel Administrators to onboard new companies by synchronizing company data and the primary contact for the Microsoft account directly from Microsoft to the AppDirect Marketplace. Company sync can take place automatically or with a single click.

This feature is relevant in the context of company migration from independent Microsoft reseller programs to the AppDirect Marketplace. For more information see Advisor, Open and CSP company migration using Microsoft multi-channel capabilities.

Procedure

Step 1. Go to Manage > Channel > Settings > Data Sync | Sync Settings.

There are 2 Company Sync settings:

  • Create companies automatically

When enabled the AppDirect Marketplace will attempt to create companies when it detects them.

If disabled the Channel Administrator will need to review which companies are to be manually created. To do this go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Pending Companies.

  • Invite admins when companies are created

When enabled the AppDirect Marketplace will automatically invite the administrator when the company is created. This applies to companies that are created automatically or manually.

If disabled the AppDirect Marketplace will not automatically invite the administrator when the company is created. In this case the Channel Administrator invites them at a later date.

When Create companies automatically is disabled and Invite admins when company created is enabled, companies are created manually and the administrator receives an invite at the moment of creation.

Select your settings and click Save.

Step 2. When companies are synced and not automatically invited, the Company sync setting Invite admins when company created is disabled, companies are held in a Pending Companies table and must be created in the AppDirect Marketplace. To do this go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Pending Companies. The Pending Companies page is displayed. The left navigation pane displays the number of pending companies.

Step 3. Select the {companyName} checkbox and click Create Selected to manually sync a company into the AppDirect Marketplace.

Once a company is created it is removed from the Pending Companies table. It is then available in Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Companies. You can start selling to the company once you have set up a valid payment method.

Known Limitations

There is currently a limitation of Company Sync that if a customer's Tenant does not have a Billing Profile with a valid email address the creation will fail. In order to remedy this please follow this guide, partner center add missing email.pdf.
