After you create a product, you can complete and modify the product listing.

​Procedure

Step 1. Go to Manage > Billing and Distribution > Products >  {Product name} | Edit > Profile & Branding > Listing info. The Manage Listing Info page opens.

Step 2. Enter information and upload files in the Manage Listing Info area. Refer to definitions after the image in completing this step.

a. Enter the company name—The company name to associate with this product. 

b. Add a product name—Identifies and brands the product on all marketplaces and portals. 

c. Add the command line name—A command-line-friendly name for the product.

d. Enter a 5 word description—Visible under the feature slogan on the feature page, it provides a description of the feature.

e. Provide a brief overview—Concise overview of the product displayed on the marketplace listing page, and via the recommendation engine. An ideal maximum length is two sentences or approximately 250 characters.

f. Enter the starting price—Manually overrides the automatically calculated starting price.

Step 3. Enter Legal information.

a. Enter a link to the Privacy Policy—The privacy policy associated with this product is shown to customers before they purchase the product.

b. Enter a link to Terms and Conditions—Customers must agree to the terms and conditions associated with this product before they complete a purchase.

Step 4. Enter a logo.

Note the following in the preceding image:

  • The high-resolution image message in the App Listing Logo and App Profile Logo sections inform you that high-resolution images are supported for your marketplace.
  • The messages also mean that you have not yet uploaded a high-resolution image for either logo.
  • The AppDirect logo is visible in each logo field. These standard resolution files were previously uploaded and are replaced when the high-resolution file is uploaded.

To upload images, use the following procedures.

a. Contact your AppDirect account representative and request that the High Resolution Images option be enabled for your marketplace.

b. Upload your App listing logo—Click Choose File, navigate to the high-resolution image you want to appear as the App Listing Logo, and then click Save. The selected file name appears next to the Choose File button. Alternatively, you can click and drag a photo into the dotted-line area. 

c. Upload your App profile logo—Click Choose File, navigate to the high-resolution image you want to appear as the App Profile Logo, and then click Save. The selected file name appears next to the Choose File button. When you click Save or Save and Preview in step 3, the image displays on your product listing. Alternatively, you can click and drag a photo into the dotted-line area. 

Step 5. Define the product category

a. Category—Categories provide a way to group similar services together. For example, all productivity and collaboration tools could be grouped within a category called Productivity. Click Chose One and select a category for your product from those available in the dropdown list.The number of categories available depends on how many your organization's Marketplace Manager has configured.

b. Sub Category—If your Marketplace Manager has configured one or more sub-categories, you can associate the product with one of them as well. Click Choose One, and select a sub-category from the dropdown list.

c. Add another category—To associate your product with an additional category, click Add another category, and follow the same procedure just described.

Step 6. When you finish managing the listing information for this product, you can take one of the following actions:

a. Click Save—After you click save, a page opens with status bars that display the progress of your product profile. As you complete the minimum setup required for tasks 1, 2, and 3, these bars display your progress. From here you can click each section to manage your product profile.

b. Click Save and Preview—This opens a preview of your product overview tab, where you can see all the information you have entered and how it appears to customers. Following is an example.

c. To make changes from here, click Edit Profile (Many areas of the product overview page can be edited from this page). Using the mouse, go to an area you want to edit. If that section is editable from this page, a pencil appears. Click the pencil and make changes as needed.
