Marketplace Managers can configure media sources and featured customers that Developers can later include in their product profiles when they are defining a product. These marketing tools are designed to build customer confidence in a product they encounter on your marketplace.

Featured customers—Recognizable companies that are customers of the Developer's product or service.

Media sources—References to product reviews or other mentions of the Developer's company or product in a blog post, newpaper or magazine article, television program, and so on. 

Note: As a Developer, you can only add customer and media references to a product after the Marketplace Manager has configured specific customers and media. Once configured, those resources are available for Developers.

Procedure

Step 1. Go to Manage > Billing and Distribution > Products > {Product name} | Edit > Profile and Branding | Customers & Media. The Customers & Media page opens.

Step 2. In the Companies Using Your App area, check all companies that you want to include as featured customers from the list that has been configured by your Marketplace Manager.

Step 3. In the Articles and Reviews area, Click Choose One, then select the media source you want to quote from the dropdown list. 

Step 4. Enter the link for the media source.

Step 5. Enter a quote from the media source or enter a description of the source you are referring to.

Step 6. If needed, click Add another media source and repeat steps 2 to 5. 

Step 7. Click Save.
