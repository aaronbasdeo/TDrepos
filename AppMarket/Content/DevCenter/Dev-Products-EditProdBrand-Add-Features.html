As a product Developer, you can add descriptive features and images to your product profile to help market your product. 

Procedure

Step 1. Go to Manage > Billing and Distribution > Products > {Product name} > Edit > Profile & Branding | Profile > Features. The Add New Feature button appears.

Step 2. Click Add New Feature. The Create New Feature page opens. 

Step 3. Enter information into all mandatory fields and, optionally, enter information in the additional fields that are available. You can also upload images to complement your product profile. The more complete your description, the more information customers have in making their purchasing decision. 

Following is a description of the fields and options available for feature creation. 

  • Title—Enter the title for your feature. This also serves as a navigation and search item when customers are searching for features. The title should provide a description of the feature. For example: Invoice creation. Note: supporting text is added afterwards in the form of characteristics. 
  • Slogan—If desired, include a memorable description of your feature.
  • Description—Describe the feature and its importance to users. This appears below the slogan on the feature page. 
  • Position—If you include more than one feature, specify the order in which each feature is displayed by selecting a position number for each feature.
  • Hide on Overview tab—Allows to you include or exclude this feature in the Features & Benefits area of the overview tab. Click the checkbox to hide the feature.
  • Image or screenshot—Upload an image that helps describe the feature and reinforces the characteristics you want to highlight for users. You can drag and drop an image, or use the Choose File button.
  • Characteristic information—For each feature, enter descriptive information to generate further customer interest in your product. Characteristics complement the feature descriptions.
  • Characteristic position—Assign a number to each characteristic to determine the order in which it is displayed.
  • Characteristic title—Enter the title that is displayed above the characteristic's description.
  • Description—Provide a description of the characteristic. 
  • Show a checkmark next to this highlight—Click to display a checkmark next to the highlight.
  • Add Highlight—Click to add another highlight to this characteristic.
  • Add another characteristic—Click to add another characteristic to further describe this feature. Add as many characteristics as needed using this process.

Step 4. After you complete your first feature, you have two choices:

a. Click Save and add another to add another feature. This opens a new Create New Feature page. Follow steps 1-3, repeating this process until your feature list is complete.  

b. Click Save and Preview to open a preview of the product's Features tab. 

c. Most areas of the Features tab can be edited from this preview page. To make changes, click Edit Profile. Using the mouse, go to an area you want to edit. If that section is editable from this page, a pencil appears. Click the pencil and make changes as needed.
