As a vendor, you need to let your marketplace customers know how they can contact your technical support organization. The information entered here appears on the Policies & Support tab of the product's profile page.

Procedure

Step 1. Go to Manage > Billing and Distribution > Products > {Product name} | Edit >, Profile & Branding | Support. This opens the Manage Support Information page.

Step 2. Enter your support organization's telephone number (including international calling code and in-country area code, as appropriate) in the Support Phone Number field.

Step 3. Enter your support organization's email address in the Support Email Address field.

Step 4. Enter a URL in the Knowledge Base/Help Center URL field. This is typically a support web page that allows customers to search content that addresses their issues, but can be any support resource that you think is useful for your marketplace customers.

Step 5. (Optional) Enter additional details in the Support Details field. For example, you could enter your support center hours, or additional phone numbers in different geographic reigons.

Step 6. Take one of the following actions:

  • Click Save. Your support contact information is saved and you remain on the Manage Support Information page. 
  • Click Save and Preview. Your support contact information is saved and the Policies & Support tab of the profile editor page opens.

 

  • If you want to make changes, click Edit Profile. Using the mouse, go to the Support area, then click the pencil. This opens the Manage Support Information page, where you can make changes.

Note: The following editing options are also available from this page (these are unrelated to support information):

  • Click the pencil where the product name is listed—Opens the Manage Listing page.
  • Click the pencil in the Terms & Conditions area—Allows you to edit the terms and conditions.
  • Click Add Screenshots in the product images area—Allows you to add another screenshot.
