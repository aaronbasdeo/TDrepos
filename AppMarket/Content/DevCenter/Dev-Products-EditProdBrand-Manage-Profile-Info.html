After you create a product, you can manage the product's profile information.

Procedure

Step 1. Go to Manage > Billing and Distribution > Products >  {Product name} | Edit > Profile & Branding > Profile. The Manage Profile Info page opens.

Step 2. Enter information and upload files in the Overview Information area.

  • a. Splash title—Define your value proposition in a few words, in a manner that catches your customer's attention.
  • b. Splash description—Enter a description that is visible under your splash title and gives your customers a deeper understanding of your product.
  • c. Overview image—Drag and drop one or more images into the upload space. This immediately saves the image. Alternatively, you can click Choose File to upload images, then navigate to the high-resolution image you want to use as the Overview image, then click Save. The selected file name appears next to the Choose File button. The image is displayed on the product profile when you complete step 5. To remove an uploaded image, click Remove. 
  • d. Embedded Video URL (optional)—Link to demo or other video resources. Only YouTube and Vimeo links are supported, and URLs must start with https://
  • e. Documentation Link (optional)—Enter a link to documentation about your product.

Step 3. Upload screenshots. 

  • a. Click Choose File, navigate to the image you want to use as a screenshot, then click Save. The selected file name appears next to the Choose File button.

​

Note: An AppDirect-configurable setting enables marketplace support for high-resolution images. It allows high-resolution mobile devices to display a 2x image (if they support it), and it also provides a default, 1x resolution image for traditional desktop devices and mobile devices that do not support 2x images.

When you upload a high-resolution image, a 2x image is stored. From it, a 1x image file is automatically created by downscaling the 2x image. The 1x file is used on devices that do not support 2x images.

  • b. Enter a caption for the screenshot. This is a label that identifies what the image is about.
  • c. If needed, you can click Add another screenshot and repeat the procedure.

Step 4. Add benefits to your product profile. Benefits are value statements designed to generate increased interest in your product. For example, how the product increases productivity for its users. 

a. Enter a benefit title. This is a compelling statement of the value of your product.

b. Enter a benefit description that supports the benefit title.

Step 5. After you finish managing the listing information for this product, you can take one of the following actions:

a. Click Save—After you click save, the Manage Profile page opens, where you can view the profile and make further edits as needed.

b. Click Save and Preview—This opens a preview of your product overview tab, where you can see all the information you have entered and how it appears to customers. Following is an example.

c. Many areas of the product overview page can be edited from this page. To make changes, click Edit Profile. Using the mouse, go to an area you want to edit. If that section is editable from this page, a pencil appears. Click the pencil and make changes as needed.
