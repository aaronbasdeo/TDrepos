Resellers

Role

Description

Assignment

reseller

Can create registered companies on a marketplace and invite their first user to register and activate an associated user account.

Can be linked manually to any number of registered companies by their respective company administrators, and then unlinked from any or all of them as needed.

A reseller can buy products and bundles — but only with permission — on behalf of any user whose company he or she created, or whose company administrator established a reseller link.

He or she has correspondingly limited authority to search for and view account and order details.

These roles have conditional authority to assign the reseller role:

reseller manager

channel administrator

corporate administrator
