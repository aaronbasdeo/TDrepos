Set a contact URL for consumer technical support

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | General Settings.

Scroll to the Support Resources area.

Enter a DNS-resolvable URL in the Contact Support URL field.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
