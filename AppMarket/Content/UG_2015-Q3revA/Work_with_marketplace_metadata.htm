Work with marketplace metadata

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Scroll to the SEO area.

(Optional) Do either or both of the following.

Do you want to edit the meta description?

Edit the meta description

Highlight and overwrite all text in the Marketplace Meta Description field.

By default, the meta description says:

AppDirect is a free web-based application. It allows you to use and manage business applications in one simple and secure site, from anywhere.

Do you want to edit the meta keywords?

Edit meta keywords

Highlight and overwrite all text in the Marketplace Meta Keywords field.

OR

Highlight and overwrite individual terms selectively.

 NOTE:  Entries in the Marketplace Meta Keywords field must be comma separated. By default, they are: AppDirect, saas, applications, marketplace, web-based apps, and business apps.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
