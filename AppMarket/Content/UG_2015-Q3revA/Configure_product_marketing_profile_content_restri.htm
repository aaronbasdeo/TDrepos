Configure product marketing profile content restrictions

See Manage sitewide product marketing profile policies[THIS DUPLICATES A TOPIC PAIR IN THE SETTINGS CHAPTER]

You can limit what vendors are authorized to expose on their product marketing profile pages and limit how consumers are authorized to interact with such pages.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality >

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

(Optional) Click through Settings | Vendors. Then, do either or both of the following.

Should Developer Center show vendors any data regarding their customers?

Allow or disallow product visibility toggling by vendors

Check or clear the Customer Access checkbox in the Vendor Portal area on the Vendors page.

Should vendors be empowered to toggle the visibility of their published products?

Allow or disallow product visibility toggling by vendors

Check or clear the Allow Vendors... checkbox in the Vendor Portal area on the Vendors page.

Click Save Settings.

(Optional) Click through Settings | Marketplace Functionality. Then, do one or more of the following in the Reviews & Questions area on the Marketplace Functionality page.

Should product profile pages show or hide user-submitted questions?

Expose or hide user-submitted questions on product profile pages

Check or clear the Questions checkbox.

Must a user consume a seat for a given product before he or she can rate or review it?

Restrict which users are authorized to rate and review a product

Check or clear the Purchase Required checkbox.

How many product ratings must users submit for a given product before its profile page shows an average of them all?

Hide a product’s ratings until the total number of submitted ratings meets or exceeds a threshold

Enter a mandatory minimum number of reviews in the At Least... field.

Should a channel administrator approve all user-submitted product questions and reviews before they are published?

Enforce or forgive manual approvals for all user-submitted content

Check or clear the User Content checkbox.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
