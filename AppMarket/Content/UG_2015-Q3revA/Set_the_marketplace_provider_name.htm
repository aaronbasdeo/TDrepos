Set the marketplace provider name

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | General Settings.

Scroll to the About area.

Enter your employer’s legal operating name in the Company Name field.

 TIP:  For example, when the provider of your marketplace is a corporation, enter the corporation’s name. Or, when the provider is a governmental agency, enter the agency’s name.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
