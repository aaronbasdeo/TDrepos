Turn vendor clickwrap prompts On or Off for oneclick products

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

 TIP:  A one-click product does not collect any billing details during checkout.

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

Scroll to the Subscriptions area.

Operate the Enable prompting the user to agree to terms and conditions checkbox, next to the Agree to Terms and Conditions Modal During One Click Purchase label, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
Even a one-click purchase prompts the user to agree to vendor terms and conditions — but only when the vendor has entered a URL for product terms and conditions during product integration.

OR

Clear the checkbox.
One-click orders do not prompt the user to agree to vendor terms and conditions

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
