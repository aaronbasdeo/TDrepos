Set a billing details collection enforcement policy for 100% discounts

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

Scroll to the Payment Requirements area.

Click one option button, next to the Require Billing Information for 100% Discounts label, as follows.

Enable — The Billing Information page is present in the purchase flow for fully discounted orders. No one can order any fully discounted product without entering his or her billing information.

Disable — The Billing Information page is removed from the purchase flow for fully discounted orders. Any registered user can order a fully discounted edition without entering his or her billing information.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
