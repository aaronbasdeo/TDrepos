Understand lead generation from referral-only products

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Products

A referral-only product is one whose product marketing profile page leads to a purchase flow outside your marketplace. This external purchase flow does not involve AppDirect.

 NOTE:  Your AppDirect server must run at least the Listing plan before referral-only integrations are available to you.
