Understand company administrator authority to invite and assign <<SUPERUSER FEATURE>>

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can turn On or Off a company administrator’s ability to assign product seats to potential users while inviting them to activate accounts on your marketplace. When this feature is turned Off, company administrators are prevented from assigning product seats to any user whose registered account is not also an activated account.
