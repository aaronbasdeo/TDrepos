Forbid updates to any active contract

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

Scroll to the Contracts area.

Make sure that the Enable advanced options for subscription changes with contracts checkbox is checked, next to the Contract Restrictions label.

Operate the Block decreases on units purchased for subscriptions with active contract checkbox, next to the Contract Restrictions label, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
Users are now prevented from reducing the number of units on all of their ACTIVE subscription contracts sitewide.

OR

Clear the checkbox.
The only time when a user is prevented from reducing the number of units on an ACTIVE contract is when the contract’s own stipulations forbid such reduction.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
