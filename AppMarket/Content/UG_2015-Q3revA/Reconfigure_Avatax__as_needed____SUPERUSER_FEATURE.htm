Reconfigure Avatax, as needed <<SUPERUSER FEATURE>>

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

 CAUTION:  Do not change Avatax entries frivolously.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

Scroll to the Third Parties area, and then edit one or both of the following fields, as appropriate.

Avatax Account — A unique identifier for your billable Avalara customer account.

Avatax License — A unique identifier for your applicable Avalara customer contract.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
