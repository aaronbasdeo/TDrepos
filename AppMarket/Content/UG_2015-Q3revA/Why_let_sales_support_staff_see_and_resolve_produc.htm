Why let sales support staff see and resolve product provisioning events?

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Roles

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that authorizes sales support representatives to view and resolve product provisioning events. Your choice here should be consistent with your vision for sales support representatives on your marketplace. Should they merely drive product sales, for example, or should they be empowered to troubleshoot and resolve problems that might occur after an assisted sale?

When this behavior is turned On, sales support representatives can see and use the same Manage Events UI that you see and use as a channel administrator.
