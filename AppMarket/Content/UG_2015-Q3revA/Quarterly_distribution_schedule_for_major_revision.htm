Quarterly distribution schedule for major revisions

We plan to distribute major revisions of this guide at (approximately) 90-day intervals. This revision cycle should help us to maintain rough parity with all of the new and changed features that our platform introduces over the weeks in a given quarter without introducing or perpetuating much slack.

Any unplanned minor revisions to this guide, distributed outside of the standard 90-day cycle, are reserved for making corrections to typographical errors, factual errors, or significant omissions.

Between major user guide distributions, channel administrators can check the weekly release notes on their respective Doc Centers to learn about any deltas between this guide and the product as we implement it in a given weekly release.
