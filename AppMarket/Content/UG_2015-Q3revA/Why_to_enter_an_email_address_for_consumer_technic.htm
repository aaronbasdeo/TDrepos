Why to enter an email address for consumer technical support

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | General Settings

You can enter the email address that listens for, and responds to, technical support requests from your marketplace consumers. Your team that responds when this address receives consumer support requests should include people with knowledge and authority to facilitate product orders, service provisioning, and billing.
