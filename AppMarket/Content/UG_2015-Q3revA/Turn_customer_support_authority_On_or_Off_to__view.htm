Turn customer support authority On or Off to ‘view as’ for technical support

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

Scroll to the Security area.

Operate the Allow channel roles to request access to customer accounts checkbox, next to the Support Access label, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
Customer support representatives are empowered to ask any registered user for access to the user’s account. Supposing that a user allows this access, the support representative can take any action on the user’s behalf. [We should be wary here of privilege escalation. Do we prevent it programmatically? If not, this feature may violate the spirit of PCI DSS certification and jeopardize or authority to process credit card transactions.]

OR

Clear the checkbox.
Customer support representatives lack any way to request access to a user account.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
