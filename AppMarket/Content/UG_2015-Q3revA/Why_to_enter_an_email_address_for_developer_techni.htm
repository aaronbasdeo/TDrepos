Why to enter an email address for developer technical support

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Vendors

You can enter a developer relations email address for your marketplace. [Is this email address visible in any page footers?]

Your team that responds when this address receives developer support requests should include people with knowledge and authority to facilitate product integrations, service provisioning, and billing.

 TIMESAVER:  Entering this URL is of no use on a marketplace that lacks a Developer Center.
