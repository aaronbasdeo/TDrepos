Set the approvals policy for assisted sales

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

Scroll to the Customer Roles area.

Click one option button, next to the Sales Agent Approval label, as follows.

Sales Agents never need customer approval — Sales agents can start and complete assisted sales without user approval.

Sales Agents always need customer approval — Sales agents must obtain user consent before completing an assisted sale.

Sales Agents can choose whether customer approval is needed — You grant sales agents the discretion to choose on a case-by-case basis whether to obtain user consent for an assisted sale.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
