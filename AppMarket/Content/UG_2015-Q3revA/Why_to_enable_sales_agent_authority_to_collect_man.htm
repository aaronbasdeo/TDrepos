Why to enable sales agent authority to collect manual payments <<SUPERUSER FEATURE>>

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that authorizes sales agents to collect payments for assisted sales outside of the billing system that your marketplace is configured to use. When the behavior is turned On and a sales agent marks an invoice as PAID, your marketplace records the charge and marks the payment as successful.

 NOTE:  In relation to assisted sales, the “sales agent” designation can mean someone with the reseller user role, when your marketplace uses the reseller service, or someone with the sales support user role.
