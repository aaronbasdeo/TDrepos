Understand user notification of assisted activation <<SUPERUSER FEATURE>>

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Most user accounts are activated manually when the corresponding user clicks a unique account-activation link in a generated email message. The point of this standard workflow is to validate that a user’s claimed email address is legitimate and that each user who joins your marketplace does so voluntarily.

Sometimes, however, a user account is both created and activated by a sales agent during the assisted sales workflow. In consideration of this nonstandard workflow, you can turn On or Off the generation of account-activation messages for accounts that were created during assisted sales.
