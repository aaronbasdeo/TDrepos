Compare product listings

Procedure

Go to https://{marketplace}/listing, where {marketplace} is the fully qualified and DNS resolvable server name for your marketplace instance.

Search, filter, and sort the list to limit which products it exposes. Then, check the Compare checkbox for each product that you hope to compare directly.

The state of the Compare button (above your filtered and sorted list) changes from dimmed to active after you designate multiple products to compare. The activated button is now responsive to clicking.

Click Compare.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
