Edit a quote

You, or a reseller manager who works for you, can edit the saved quotes that your sales agents generate.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Quotes > {quote} > [gear icon ()] > Edit a Quote

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Quotes.

Click through Billing | Quotes.

Click a quote to see its details on the Manage Quote page.

Choose Edit Quote from the gear icon ().

 NOTE:  A quote might be in any of these possible states: Pending reseller manager approval, pending purchase, pending user approval, or pending final review.

(Optional) Do any of the following.

Do you want to accept the price quote?

Accept a quote

Choose Accept Quote from the gear icon.

Depending on the outcome, a system message indicates either success or failure. You can dismiss the message.

Dismiss the system message.

Do you want to reject the price quote?

Reject a quote

Choose Reject Quote from the gear icon.

A system message asks you, “Are you sure you want to reject this quote? The submitter will receive a notification that the quote has been denied.”

Click Yes.

Do you want to send a wholesale price to the sales agent?

Share wholesale pricing

Choose Send Wholesale Price from the gear icon.

Depending on the outcome, a system message indicates either success or failure. You can dismiss the message.

Dismiss the system message.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
