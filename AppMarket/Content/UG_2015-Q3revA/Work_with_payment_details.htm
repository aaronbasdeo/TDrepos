Work with payment details

You can explore the fine-grained details of any payment, in isolation from all other payments.

 NOTE:  Although a UI toggle above the Payments table labels its two view states Overview and Details (), the latter view is not what we mean in this guide when we mention “payment details.” Instead, we mean the modal popup that opens — which describes only one payment record in detail — when you click through one row in the Payments table, regardless of that table’s toggled view state when you click it.

Relative to your payments ledger:

  •   The Overview toggle exposes seven values per payment record.
  •   The Details toggle exposes 11 values.
  •   These two views share five of their values in common. So:

         –   Two values per record are unique to the Overview view.
         –   Six values per record are unique to the Details view.

Choose whichever view exposes more values that matter to you.

  •   Values that both views expose are Payment ID, Date, Customer, Vendor, and Total.
  •   Unique values in the Overview view are Payment Method and Payment Result.
  •   Unique values in the Details view are Payout Status, Invoice ID, Product, Edition, Description,
       and Vendor Share.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Payments > {payment}

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

Click past Billing | Payments.

Click a Payments table row whose underlying record you want to review.

A modal popup opens, which describes the corresponding payment in full.

(Optional) Do any of the following.

Do you want to know what billed party remitted the payment?

Check who remitted the payment

Look in the page heading’s far-left column, under the marketplace provider logo.

Do you want to see the payment processing date?

Check when the payment was processed

Look in the page heading’s far-right column, next to the Payment date label.

Do you want to see exactly what amount was paid?

Check what amount was paid

Look in the page heading’s far-right column, next to the Total Paid label.

Do you want to know which invoices the payment covers?

Check which invoices applied the payment

Look all throughout the Invoice ID column in the Invoices Paid table.

 NOTE:  When the Invoices Paid table contains more records than the Invoice Details modal can render all together at once, your marketplace causes the table to span multiple pages. In this case, you can use pagination controls under the table to navigate from one page to another. Each such page contains additional rows, and each row cites an invoice (in the Invoice ID column) that applied some part of the modal’s described payment.

Do you want to refund all or part of the payment?

Refund all or part of a disputed or erroneous payment

Click Refund Payment in the page heading’s far-right column.

The Refund Payment modal opens.

Check each line item that should be refunded.

You must check at least one line item.

(Optional) Enter an explanation for the refund.

Any explanation that you enter is then visible on all UI renderings of the corresponding payment record.

Click Refund.

The Refund Payment modal closes.

Do you want to make note of a refund that you already issued outside of the AppDirect platform?

Save a notation that payment occurred outside your marketplace

Click Enter Manual Refund in the page heading’s far-right column.

The Enter Manual Refund modal opens.

Check each line item that you refunded previously, outside the AppDirect platform.

You must check at least one line item.

(Optional) Enter an explanation for the refund.

Any explanation that you enter is then visible on all UI renderings of the corresponding payment record.

Click Refund.

The Enter Manual Refund modal closes.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
