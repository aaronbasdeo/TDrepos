Why let resellers disable linked companies?

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Roles

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that authorizes resellers to disable every user account that is associated with any of the reseller’s linked companies. Your choice here should be consistent with your vision for reseller operations on your marketplace. Should resellers merely drive product sales, for example, or do you want them to manage their linked companies?
