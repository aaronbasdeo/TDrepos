Set a 30-day spending limit per company

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

Scroll to the Purchase Restrictions area.

Operate the Set spend limit checkbox, next to the 30 Day Spend Limit label, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
The per 30 days field is exposed. Enter a maximum amount.

OR

Clear the checkbox.
Any limit that was enforced previously is no longer enforced.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
