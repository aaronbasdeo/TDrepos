Intended audiences

At AppDirect, our namesake platform is multi-sided, serving the overlapping interests of roughly two dozen distinct user roles. So, various parts of this user guide collect together the chapters that address each of its various audiences. This guide’s mandate is to mentor one AppDirect audience at a time toward deepened expertise. See User role basics, to learn about AppDirect-standard, assignable user roles that your marketplace might support.

Our plan is to develop and release revisions to this overarching guide roughly every 90 days, until sufficient material is present to address and engage each of its targeted audiences.
