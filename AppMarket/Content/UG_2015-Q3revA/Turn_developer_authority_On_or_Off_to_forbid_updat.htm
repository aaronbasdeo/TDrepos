Turn developer authority On or Off to forbid updates to active contracts

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Billing Settings | Billing Functionality.

Scroll to the Contracts area.

Operate the Allow vendors to configure contract restrictions per price checkbox, next to the Contract Restrictions label, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
Developers can see and use UI elements to forbid changing how many consumable units an active contract stipulates.

OR

Clear the checkbox.
Developers cannot forbid changes to how many consumable units an active contract stipulates.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
