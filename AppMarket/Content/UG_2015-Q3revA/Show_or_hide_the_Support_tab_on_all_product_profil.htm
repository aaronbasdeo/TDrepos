Show or hide the Support tab on all product profiles

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

Scroll to the Customer UI area.

Operate the Hide support content for all products checkbox, next to the Product Support label, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
The Support tab is suppressed sitewide on all product profile pages.

OR

Clear the checkbox.
Product profile pages includes a Support tab when their vendor entered any support details during product integration.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
