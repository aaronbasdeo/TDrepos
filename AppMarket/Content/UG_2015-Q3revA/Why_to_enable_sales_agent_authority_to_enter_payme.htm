Why to enable sales agent authority to enter payment details

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that authorizes sales agents to enter a target user’s billing details during an assisted sale. [When the behavior is turned Off, can an assisted sale occur only after the target user has entered his or her own billing details, so the marketplace has them on record?]

 NOTE:  In relation to assisted sales, the “sales agent” designation can mean someone with the reseller user role, when your marketplace uses the reseller service, or someone with the sales support user role.
