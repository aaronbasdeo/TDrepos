Work with the revenue shares history for a product

You can explore the fine-grained details of any one product’s revenue sharing history, in isolation from any other product’s equivalent history.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Billing | Reconciliation (Revenue Shares) > {product}

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Marketplace.

Click past Billing | Reconciliation, and then scroll to the Revenue Shares table.

Click the row that summarizes the current revenue sharing agreement for one product in particular.

A modal popup opens, which cites the described product’s current and past revenue-sharing percentages per participant and identifies by name the registered user who was responsible for editing any of their published revisions.

Click Download CSV to store a local copy of the described product’s revenue-share percentages over time. [No other task is possible here, right?]

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
