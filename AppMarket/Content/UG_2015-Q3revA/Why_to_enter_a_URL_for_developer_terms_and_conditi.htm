Why to enter a URL for developer terms and conditions

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Vendors

You can enter the public URL for marketplace terms and conditions that apply only to developers. Setting this URL is optional. However, when it is set, registered developers must agree to the policy when they create products in your Developer Center. [Is there a link to the policy in any page footers?] [Do we audit compliance?]

 TIMESAVER:  Entering this URL is of no use on a marketplace that lacks a Developer Center.
