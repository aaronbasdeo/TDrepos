Approve or deny product publication approval requests

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Products > Catalog | Product Catalog

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Products.

Click through Catalog | Product Catalog.

When registered developers have submitted any launch requests that you haven’t yet approved or denied, we show a call to action, inline, on the Product Catalog page.

Click the bold text to load a list of all products whose publication request awaits your response.

(Optional) Click Show Filters to expand a toolbar whose controls help you to filter which products the table should show.

 NOTE:  Filter selections take effect immediately. Each effect that does not negate a previous selection is additive.

Use any combination of filters until you find the products that matter to you.

Choose Pending from the Status list.

Choose an option from the Product Type list. Supported options are Show All, Static, Standing Cloud [soon to be Private App?], AppDirect Service, Cloud Infrastructure and Services, Domain Provider, or Web App. [Am I right to believe some of these should not be exposed externally?]

Choose an option from the Usage Type list. Supported options are Show All, Multiple Users, and Single User.

Choose an option from the Purchase Type list. Supported options are Show All, Buyable, Coming Soon, and Referral. [I’d expect the only valid selection here to be Coming Soon, but I believe that state can be toggled On or Off per marketplace and therefore should not be represented here as the only valid choice. Correct?]

(Optional) Enter a query in the Search field. Then, either press Enter or click the magnifying glass icon.

(Optional) Click a product name to load its marketing profile page, where you can learn more about it and assess its suitability for your marketplace.

(Optional) Do one of the following.

Click Edit to adjust any product settings or product marketing profile claims that are not suitable.

Click either Approve or Deny.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
