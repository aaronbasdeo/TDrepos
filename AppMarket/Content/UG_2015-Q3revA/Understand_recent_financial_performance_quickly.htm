Understand recent financial performance quickly

Navigation path:

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Dashboard

Your marketplace dashboard shows a key performance indicator (KPI) digest and three gauges that summarize the financial performance of your marketplace per week, month, quarter, or year — sometimes alongside calls to action.

The facts that underlie your dashboard gauges are exportable to CSV.

 NOTE:  Dashboard calls to action, when present, do not pertain to financial settings or transactions. Instead, they pertain to your open action items on other Marketplace Manager pages. For example, they might prompt you to investigate and resolve stalled provisioning of a consumer’s product access.
