Turn reseller authority On or Off to cancel the subscriptions of their linked companies

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Roles

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | General Settings.

Scroll to the Reseller Permissions area.

Operate the Cancel subscription toggle, as follows.

Slide the toggle right () to turn On the described behavior.

OR

Slide the toggle left () to turn Off the described behavior.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
