Configure company administrator authority to invite and assign <<SUPERUSER FEATURE>>

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

Scroll to the Customer UI area.

Operate the Allow assigning apps to users who have not activated their accounts checkbox, next to the Invite and Assign label, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
Company administrators can now assign products to invited new users.

OR

Clear the checkbox.
Company administrators must wait until a registered user account is activated before assigning any products to it.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
