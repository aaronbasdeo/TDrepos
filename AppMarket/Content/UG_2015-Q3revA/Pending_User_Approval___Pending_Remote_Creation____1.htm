The sequence of transitions in Scenario 20

The chronology of transitions in this scenario is as follows.

Order and subscription state transitions in Scenario 20

 

A newly placed order starts its life in the PENDING USER APPROVAL state when a reseller (or other authorized representative of your marketplace) has placed an order on behalf of a registered user.

There is no subscription state at this stage. A subscription does not yet exist.

 

Transition to a PENDING REMOTE CREATION state occurs after the registered user consents to this assisted purchase — and after your marketplace notifies the product vendor that an order was placed, which requires provisioning (here, the requested edition is billable) — and when all of these statements are true simultaneously:

The order specifies a hosted product whose vendor must provision it.

The order should take effect immediately, and therefore is not post-dated.

The order does not apply any discount.

The subscription state is INITIALIZED.

 

Transition to an ACTIVE state occurs when the relevant vendor finishes provisioning. The consumer is now truly subscribed to a billable product that carries a recurring billing cycle.

The subscription state is ACTIVE.

 

Transition to a SUSPENDED state occurs when an invoice is not paid — and, in the case of a product edition that allows a grace period for late payments, remains unpaid all through its grace period. But do also consider the following:

Payment of the past-due sum during the same billing cycle in which it was due restores the SUSPENDED order once again to its properly ACTIVE state.

Payment of the past-due sum during any billing cycle after the one in which it was due sets the original purchase order to a FINISHED state and spawns a replacement purchase order in the ACTIVE state.

After an order is SUSPENDED, which describes a product edition that does not renew automatically, the consumer’s manual renewal set the original purchase order to a FINISHED state and spawn a replacement purchase order in the ACTIVE state.

The subscription state is SUSPENDED.

 

Upon payment, the suspended order returns to its ACTIVE state.

The subscription state is ACTIVE.
