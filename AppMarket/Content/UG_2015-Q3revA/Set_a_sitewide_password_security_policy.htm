Set a sitewide password security policy

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

Click a Password Policy option in the Security area to choose it.

Strict — Each registered user account must be secured with a password that is at least 10 characters long and matches for this pattern:

contains at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)

contains at least one lowercase letter (a-z)

contains at least one digit (0-9) or special character (~!@#$%^&*()_+=<>?)

In the strict policy, passwords expire automatically and must be changed when they age to 90 days. Depending on how your marketplace is configured, it might notify users through email when their passwords are scheduled to expire.

Moderate — Each registered user account must be secured with a password that is at least 8 characters long and matches for this pattern:

contains at least one letter (A-Z or a-z)

contains at least one digit (0-9) or special character (~!@#$%^&*()_+=<>?)

In the moderate policy, passwords expire automatically and must be changed when they age to 90 days. Depending on how the marketplace is configured, it might notify users through email when their passwords are scheduled to expire.

Default — (The most lenient policy) Each registered user account must be secured with a password that is at least 8 characters long and matches for this pattern:

contains at least one letter (A-Z or a-z)

contains at least one digit (0-9) or special character (~!@#$%^&*()_+=<>?)

In the default policy, passwords do not expire automatically.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
