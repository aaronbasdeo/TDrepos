A 30-day spending limit can apply per company

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Billing Settings | Billing Functionality

You can set a hard limit on how much each company can spend on your marketplace during any 30day period. [Is this correct? I ask because neither 365 days nor 366 days in a leap year is divisible in full by 30. The longer a company was registered on a marketplace, the more its 30-day cycle would drift away from calendar months. And no two companies would necessarily track to the same 30-day cycle.]
