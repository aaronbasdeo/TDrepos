Signposting

Some paragraphs in this guide are flagged to communicate their very purpose at a glance.

 WARNING:  Warnings tell you about factors affecting systems, which could prevent basic operation or violate service level agreements. Warnings are CRITICALLY IMPORTANT.

 CAUTION:  Cautions tell you about factors affecting data, which could disrupt expected operations or violate service level agreements. Cautions are URGENTLY IMPORTANT.

 NOTE:  Notes deliver essential information about situational factors and contingencies that could interfere with your success, or they explain situational factors that could otherwise distract or misdirect you. Notes are IMPORTANT.

 TIMESAVER:  Timesavers tell you about shortcuts or other ways to accelerate your work toward a goal. Timesavers are GOOD TO KNOW.

 TIP:  Tips highlight nonessential information that, nonetheless, might improve your product satisfaction or effectiveness. Tips tell you about small but useful details that you could easily miss or forget. Tips are GOOD TO KNOW.
