Set a terms and conditions modification date for developers <<SUPERUSER FEATURE>>

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Vendors

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Vendors.

Scroll to the Support area.

Use the Developer terms updated on calender tool to choose and set the modification date.

(Optional) Click Clear to reset the calendar and clear any date that you set previously.

(Optional) Click << to redraw the calendar with dates from 1 month earlier.

(Optional) Click >> to redraw the calendar with dates from 1 month later.

Click a date to choose and set it as the modification date.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
