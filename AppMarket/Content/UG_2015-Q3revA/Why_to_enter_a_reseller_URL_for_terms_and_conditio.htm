Why to enter a reseller URL for terms and conditions

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can enter the public URL for marketplace terms and conditions that apply only to resellers. Setting this URL is optional. However, when it is set, registered resellers must agree to the policy when they activate their accounts on your marketplace. [Is there a link to the policy in any page footers?] [Do we audit compliance?]

 TIMESAVER:  Entering this URL is of no use on a marketplace that lacks a Reseller Center.
