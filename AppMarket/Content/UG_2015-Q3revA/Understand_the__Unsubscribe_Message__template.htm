Understand the ‘Unsubscribe Message’ template

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Custom UI | Customer Emails

Several notification message templates inject into their generated messages a short text string that they all share in common, which you can either edit in isolation from the templates that call upon it, or never edit. Until and unless you edit it, the text string is always, literally, the word “Unsubscribe.”

This text is the clickable, human-readable representation of a lengthy, machine-generated link in the message footer, for templates whose recipients can unsubscribe from their future output.
