Understand billing information on a company details page

Navigation path:

Manage > Channel > Marketplace > Home | Customers > [Browse by Company] > {company}

When you click through any company record on the Companies page, Marketplace Manager loads details about just that company. From a financial standpoint, these details communicate:

What tax rate applies in the company’s physical locale

Exactly how much money the company has spent thus far through your marketplace

Real-time counts of all the company’s:

free trials

expired free trials

purchased products

suspended products

unpaid invoices

In addition, a series of inline tables on each company details page help you to search, filter, and track records of all individual orders, invoices, and payments that are associated with the described company.
