Marketplace administrators

Role

Description

Assignment

reseller administrator

Oversees all resellers and their links to customers and orders on the platform.

A reseller administrator must be part of a company whose “Channel Access” flag is enabled.

Only a channel administrator or a corporate administrator can assign the reseller administrator role.

channel administrator

Has near-total authority over one marketplace instance, and near-total access to its data.

This role can manage and oversee virtually every detail of its strategy and daily operation, subject to any governance by a corporate administrator.

Corporate administrators alone have the authority to assign the channel administrator role.

corporate administrator

Has total authority over multiple marketplaces, overseeing them centrally on behalf of their corporate parent.

This role can overrule a channel administrator's actions on any child marketplace that is subject to its authority, and can perform any action that a channel administrator has authority to perform.

Corporate administrators alone have the authority to assign the corporate administrator role.
