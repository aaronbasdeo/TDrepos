Why to enable sales agent authority to collect CVV details <<SUPERUSER FEATURE>>

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

You can turn a marketplace behavior On or Off that authorizes sales agents to enter the target user’s credit card CVV code during an assisted sale. When the behavior is turned Off, the CVV field does not accept any text entry. [Supposing that this behavior is turned Off and a credit card payment must be processed, who enters the CVV code, and when, and where?]

 NOTE:  In relation to assisted sales, the “sales agent” designation can mean someone with the reseller user role, when your marketplace uses the reseller service, or someone with the sales support user role.
