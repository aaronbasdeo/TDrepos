Work with your products in the Blocked state

Procedure

Go to MyApps.

Do one or more of the following.

Do you want to request an entitlement?

Request a product

Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

<outcome>

Choose Request App.

<outcome>

Do you want to start a free trial?

Start a free trial

Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile.

<outcome>

Choose Activate Free Trial.

<outcome>

Do you want to install an extension?

Install an extension

Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

<outcome>

Choose Install Extension.

<outcome>

Do you want to enter product login credentials?

Add your product credentials

Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

<outcome>

Choose Add Credentials.

<outcome>

Enter your credentials. Then, click Save.

Do you want to configure a product’s settings?

Configure an app

Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

<outcome>

Choose Configure App.

<outcome>

Do you want to download the product’s client software for local installation?

Download an app

Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

<outcome>

Choose Download App.

<outcome>

Do you want to renew the subscription?

Renew your subscription

Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

<outcome>

Choose Renew Subscription.

<outcome>

Do you want to delete the product from your MyApps page?

Remove an app

Allow your pointer to hover over the top third of the product tile to expose its settings menu.

<outcome>

Choose Remove App.

<outcome>

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
