Turn sales agent authority On or Off to collect manual payments <<SUPERUSER FEATURE>>

Navigation path

Manage > Channel > Settings > Settings | Marketplace Functionality

Before you begin

Log in to your marketplace as its administrator.

Procedure

Go to Manage > Channel > Settings.

Click past Settings | Marketplace Functionality.

Scroll to the Customer Roles area.

Operate the Enable manual payments in assisted sales purchase flow checkbox, next to the Assisted Sales label, as follows.

Check the checkbox.
<<The described behavior is enabled>>.

OR

Clear the checkbox.
<<The described behavior is disabled>>.

Click Save Settings.

Stop. You have completed this procedure.
